About Master Data

Master data forms the foundation of your CRM system. These reference tables ensure data consistency and provide standardized options for:

  • Lead Creation: Required fields for customer sectors, company types, and business categories
  • Contact Management: Standardized roles and designations
  • Lead Sources: Track where your leads originate
  • Service Offerings: Manage your service catalog

lightbulb Tip: Keep master data up to date to ensure smooth Lead Creation workflow and accurate reporting.
